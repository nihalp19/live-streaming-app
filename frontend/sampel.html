How to Use This Service

  1. To broadcast: Enter a unique stream ID and click "Start Broadcasting". Allow camera and microphone access when prompted.
  2. To watch: Enter the stream ID of the broadcast you want to watch and click "Watch Stream".
  3. Share your stream ID with others so they can watch your broadcast.
  4. Use the "Stop" buttons to end your broadcast or stop watching a stream.
